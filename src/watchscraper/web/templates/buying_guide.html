Buying guide

A quantitative screen for your next purchase — families ranked by dip, price stability, exit liquidity, and discount to dealer ask. A screen, not gospel: always inspect the specific watch.

Score components: recent dip = negative 4-week momentum (buying weakness, not chasing); stable value = low annualized volatility of weekly medians; liquid market = healthy 5–25% ask/sold spread with real sales volume; sold below dealer ask = realized prices far under Chrono24 asking, i.e. private-sale discount. Minimum 30 clean sales required.
