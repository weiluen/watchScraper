Buying guide

Ranked for a buyer who doesn't want to lose money: low round-trip cost (what you lose buying at ask and selling at market), a smoothed value trend that isn't bleeding, and a deep enough market to exit. A screen, not gospel: always inspect the specific watch.

Score components: round-trip cost = Chrono24 dealer ask over realized sold prices — the loss you take buying at ask and selling at market the same day; value trend = 1-month change of the smoothed, composition-adjusted model value (not raw weekly medians); deep market = 100+ clean sales, so the marked value is real and you can exit near it. Minimum 30 clean sales required.
